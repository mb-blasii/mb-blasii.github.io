C!AO Piazza Navona

Interactive Installation · Urban Experience · VR Experience

Project Overview

C!AO – Piazza Navona Experience is an immersive urban XR installation designed to blend digital interaction with one of Rome’s most iconic public spaces.

The experience invites visitors to explore Piazza Navona through a playful and interactive layer, mixing cultural heritage with real-time digital feedback.


My contribution covered experience design, interaction flow, technical development, and optimization for standalone VR platforms.

Features

  • VR Urban Experience Blends virtual interaction with a real-world cultural landmark.
  • Meta Quest Native Optimized for standalone VR with smooth performance and fast onboarding.
  • Public Space Engagement Designed to encourage curiosity and playful exploration in a social context.
  • Site-Specific Design Tailored specifically for Piazza Navona’s spatial and cultural identity.
  • Lightweight Technical Setup Minimal hardware requirements for easy deployment in public installations.
  • Experience-Driven Game Design Focused on atmosphere, discovery, and emotional impact rather than traditional gameplay loops.
