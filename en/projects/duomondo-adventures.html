Duomondo Adventures

Mobile Game · Geo-Tag Exploration · Unity

Project Overview

Duomondo Adventures is a location-based exploration game that blends real-world discovery with light narrative elements and quest progression.


Players are encouraged to explore their surroundings, unlock story fragments, and interact with geo-tagged content placed in real-world locations.

The game encourages exploration and discovery of cultural Points Of Interest (POIs) such as parks, museums and public spaces through a fantasy world narrative.


The game was released exclusively in Italy. I worked as a Unity Developer on gameplay systems, exploration mechanics, and mobile performance optimization.

Features

  • Geo-Tagged Exploration Gameplay elements are tied to real-world locations, encouraging outdoor discovery.
  • GeoSpatial Integration GPS and POIs locations optimized for accurate positioning and performance using Google GeoSpatial data.
  • Light Narrative Progression Story beats and world-building are unlocked through player exploration.
  • Map-Centric Interface Interactive map view guiding players toward nearby points of interest.
  • Mobile-First UX Designed for quick sessions, touch input, and on-the-go play.
  • Performance & Battery Optimization Carefully tuned systems for GPS usage and background processes.
