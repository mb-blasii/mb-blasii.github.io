VR Graffiti Game

Virtual Reality · Interactive Art · Game Concept

Project Overview

VR Graffiti Game is a virtual reality game concept focused on creative expression through immersive street-art mechanics.
Choose if you want to be an artist or a thug, and explore a stylized urban environment where you can create graffiti art using VR controllers.


Players can paint, tag, and customize virtual environments using intuitive VR interactions, blending art with playful gameplay systems.

Screenshots

Features

  • Simple Painting System Spray, draw, and tag in full 3D space using natural VR gestures.
    Choose a design from the artbook and paint it over the walls.
  • Intuitive VR Controls Designed around comfort and accessibility for easy gameplay.
  • Layer Separation Each graffiti is subdivided into color layers according to texture data. Choose the spray can between RED, GREEN, BLUE, BLACK and WHITE to paint each layer.
  • Gameplay Scenario Paint over the walls of the city while avoiding patrolling police men. Explore the urban environment to find hidden walls and secret areas to tag and acquire points and new designs.
  • Modular Environments Virtual spaces can be adapted to different themes and artistic scenarios.
  • Concept-to-Prototype Workflow Built to quickly validate ideas and mechanics in immersive contexts.
