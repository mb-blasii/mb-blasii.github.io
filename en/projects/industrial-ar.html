Industrial AR Application

AR · Industrial Tools · Real-Time Visualization

Project Overview

Augmented reality solution designed to support industrial workflows through real-time visual guidance and data overlays on smartphones and smart glasses.


The system enhances on-site operations by allowing technicians and operators to access contextual information directly in their field of view, improving efficiency and reducing human error. The software was made to display 3D models, data and analysis maps on-site retrieveing information from cloud services.


I worked on interaction design, real-time visualization systems, and performance optimization for stable and safe use in production environments.
