NASA Rover Experience

Interactive Simulation · VR Systems · Educational Experience

Project Overview

NASA Rover Experience is an interactive simulation designed to let users build the rover and learn about its components to explore the red planet and retrieve mineral samples.


The experience focuses on realism, accessibility, and educational impact, combining accurate scientific references with engaging real-time interaction.

Screenshots

Features

  • Immersive Rover Simulation Hands-on control of a virtual rover with mechanics inspired by real exploration missions.
  • Educational Design Built to communicate scientific concepts in a clear and engaging way.
  • Real-Time Interaction Systems Implementation of navigation, obstacle handling, and task-based objectives.
  • System Architecture Modular gameplay framework enabling fast iteration and feature scalability.
  • User-Centered Experience Designed with accessibility and clarity in mind for a wide audience.
